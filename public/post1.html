Hello Pandaemonium!

September 16, 2020 by Code Devil

Hello and welcome to my new website! This will be my great project for my time waiting for the world to open up again. I have found my self recently graduated from college and with tons of time on my hands. The economy is in tatters and honestly I am a free spirit. I figure now is as good a time as any to try on the hat of blogger and developer. So here I am.

For my first project of note I am going to be working on an action rpg. Nothing too crazy. Just a basic forest with some small towns and a couple dungeons. Going to be focusing on working on developing the ground work for something larger. I am hoping to involve crafting, nightly wave survival, body horror, eldritch creatures, and dream walking. So yeah I will see how that goes.

Aside from development stuff everynow and then I try my hand at writing so I may write an opinion piece or two. I have been looking at the topics of religion, ethics, and futurism. Specifically looking at The Satanic Temply, Sentientism, and more generally seeking to free myself from the confines of what nature has manifested me as.

So if any of my planned content sounds interesting feel free to follow this site on social media for updates on posts!
